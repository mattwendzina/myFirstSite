About

Background

I am a creative person who always loves to be learning new things. I trained formally trained in music and took up a career in music teaching, both as a guitar tutor and as a classroom music teacher. In 2014, I had the chance to move to the United States to take a teaching position at a small private school in Fayetteville, Arkansas. This was an incredible time where I learned so much and made life-long friendships.

At the end of 2015 I returned to the UK and made the decision to take a break from teaching. Over the following two years I did a wide variety of jobs, as well as training as a photographer and running a small event/wedding photography business for a year. At the beginning of 2018 I decided I wanted to pursue something new and began to look into web development. Over the past 12 months I have been teaching myself to code and have since been lucky to get a lot of practice at the basics in my last job as a content manager/developer.
